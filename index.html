Fast Fashion is killing the planet

Fast fashion is the 2nd largest producer of waste in the world.

Our Goal:

Fast fashion is a business model which capitalizes on changing trends and low production costs while sacrificing the good of the environment and worker safety. There are many ways to tackle this issue, but this website will aim to provide consumers options of what to do with their old clothing. It is important for all consumers to know what happens to manufactured clothing that is thrown away or donated in the end.

Random Fact Generator:

85% of the clothing Americans consume (nearly 3.8 billion pounds annually) is sent to landfills amounting to 80 pounds per American per year
